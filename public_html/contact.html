Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common inquiries

How do I contact creator support?

If you're a creator with questions about launching or managing your project, you can email us at creators@yallastarter.com or call our creator support line at +966 11 234 5678. Our team is available Sunday through Thursday, 9am to 6pm.

I'm having issues with my payment. Who should I contact?

For payment-related issues, please email support@yallastarter.com with details of the problem you're experiencing. Include your order number and the project name for faster assistance. Our payments team will respond within 24 hours.

How can I arrange a meeting with your team?

If you'd like to schedule a meeting with our team, please fill out the contact form on this page with your preferred date and time, or email support@yallastarter.com. We offer both in-person meetings at our offices and virtual meetings via video conference.

Do you offer partnership opportunities?

Yes, we're always open to exploring partnership opportunities with organizations that share our mission of supporting Saudi innovation. Please email support@yallastarter.com with details about your organization and your partnership proposal.
