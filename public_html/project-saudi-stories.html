About Saudi Stories

Saudi Stories is a groundbreaking documentary series that takes viewers on an intimate journey through the Kingdom's diverse cultural landscape. Each episode focuses on a different region, tradition, or aspect of Saudi life, revealing the stories of ordinary people who are the keepers of extraordinary heritage.

From the ancient art of falconry in the northern regions to the traditional coffee ceremonies of the south, from the bustling souks of Jeddah to the serene oases of the Empty Quarter, this series captures the essence of what it means to be Saudi in the 21st century while honoring the deep roots that connect the present to the past.
