About Mohammed

I'm a passionate entrepreneur and innovator based in Riyadh, with a background in renewable energy engineering and a deep commitment to sustainable development in Saudi Arabia.

After completing my Master's degree in Sustainable Energy Systems at King Abdullah University of Science and Technology (KAUST), I worked for several years in the energy sector before deciding to pursue my own ventures that align with Saudi Vision 2030's goals of economic diversification and environmental sustainability.

Through YallaStarter, I've been able to bring several of my ideas to life while also supporting other creative projects that contribute to Saudi Arabia's cultural and economic development. I believe in the power of community-driven innovation and the importance of preserving our cultural heritage while embracing technological advancement.
