About the Platform

The Saudi Heritage Digital Art Platform is a pioneering project aimed at preserving the rich cultural heritage of the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia through advanced digital technology. This platform combines traditional art with modern technology to create immersive interactive experiences that allow visitors to explore Saudi heritage in an innovative and engaging way.

Through the use of virtual reality, augmented reality, and 3D modeling technologies, the platform allows visitors to explore historical sites, cultural artifacts, and Saudi traditions as if they were physically present. This project is not just a display of heritage, but a bridge connecting the past, present, and future.

Platform Features

Virtual Reality Tours

Explore Saudi historical sites and cultural artifacts through immersive high-resolution virtual tours.

3D Modeling

Accurate 3D models of historical artifacts and sites with interactive exploration capabilities.

Digital Storytelling

Interactive stories that narrate the history and culture of the Kingdom in an engaging and understandable way for all ages.

Mobile Application

Mobile app available on all platforms with augmented reality features and social interaction.

Interactive Experiences

Interactive activities that allow users to participate in heritage preservation and knowledge sharing.

Global Access

Available in both Arabic and English with worldwide accessibility from anywhere in the world.
