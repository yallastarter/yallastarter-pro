About NovaMind Inc.

Building globally scalable, AI-driven digital platforms and innovation ecosystems tailored to local markets

Who We Are

NovaMind Inc. is a U.S. Corporation specializing in building globally scalable, AI-driven digital platforms that create innovation ecosystems tailored to local markets. We develop comprehensive solutions that empower entrepreneurs, foster creativity, and drive sustainable economic growth.

Our focus on Saudi Arabia represents our commitment to supporting emerging markets with cutting-edge technology and localized approaches. We understand that true innovation requires more than just technology—it requires understanding local culture, values, and aspirations.

Through our flagship platforms, YallaStarter and Mind71, we're building not just applications, but a national innovation infrastructure that aligns with Saudi Arabia's Vision 2030 goals and creates lasting impact for generations to come.

Our Mission & Vision

The driving force behind everything we do

Our Mission

To democratize innovation by giving creators equal access to funding, mentorship, and digital tools, regardless of their background or connections.

Our Vision

To be the leading global enabler of entrepreneurship and sustainable growth, starting with Saudi Arabia's Vision 2030 goals.

Empowerment

We empower Saudi youth, women entrepreneurs, and SMEs by providing them with the tools and opportunities they need to succeed.

ESG Commitment

We are committed to social inclusion, cultural continuity, environmental sustainability, and governance transparency in all our operations.

Innovation Infrastructure

We build not just platforms, but comprehensive national innovation infrastructure that supports long-term economic growth.

Global Scalability

Our AI-driven platforms are designed to scale globally while maintaining deep local market understanding and cultural sensitivity.

Our Innovations

NovaMind Inc. has developed two flagship platforms that are revolutionizing how innovation happens in Saudi Arabia and beyond. These platforms work together to create a comprehensive ecosystem for entrepreneurs and creators.

Our Flagship Platforms

Revolutionary tools that are transforming innovation in Saudi Arabia

YallaStarter

Saudi Arabia's First Reward-Based Crowdfunding Platform

Fully localized, Shariah-compliant, and aligned with Vision 2030, YallaStarter democratizes access to funding for Saudi creators and entrepreneurs. Our platform connects innovative projects with supportive backers, creating a vibrant ecosystem of creativity and innovation.

Mind71

Embedded Arabic-First AI Advisor

Our advanced AI system provides feasibility assessments, grant-matching, and Vision 2030 KPI mapping. Mind71 serves as an intelligent advisor that helps entrepreneurs make informed decisions and align their projects with national development goals.
