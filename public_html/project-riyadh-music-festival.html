About the Festival

The Riyadh Music Festival is a groundbreaking cultural event that celebrates the diverse musical heritage of Saudi Arabia. This three-day festival brings together traditional folk musicians, contemporary artists, and international performers to create an unforgettable experience that bridges the gap between the Kingdom's rich past and vibrant present.

Set in the heart of Riyadh's cultural district, the festival will feature multiple stages, interactive workshops, food vendors showcasing local cuisine, and art installations that tell the story of Saudi music through the ages. From the haunting melodies of traditional Najdi music to the innovative sounds of modern Saudi artists, this festival promises to be a celebration of creativity, culture, and community.
