About The Abyss

The Abyss represents a revolutionary leap in entertainment technology, combining the thrill of high-speed rollercoasters with the mystique of underground exploration. This groundbreaking project will create the world's largest underground rollercoaster, featuring state-of-the-art engineering and immersive storytelling that takes riders on an unforgettable journey through ancient caverns and modern marvels.

Located in the heart of Saudi Arabia's entertainment district, The Abyss will feature a 2.5-kilometer track that descends 150 meters below ground level, passing through natural rock formations and specially designed underground chambers. The experience includes cutting-edge lighting, sound systems, and special effects that create an otherworldly atmosphere.

Project Features

Underground Experience

Descend 150 meters below ground through natural rock formations and specially designed chambers.

High-Speed Thrills

Experience speeds up to 120 km/h with advanced magnetic propulsion technology.

Immersive Lighting

State-of-the-art LED lighting system creates dynamic visual experiences throughout the ride.

3D Audio System

Surround sound technology provides immersive audio that enhances the underground experience.

Safety First

Advanced safety systems and emergency protocols ensure a secure experience for all riders.

Eco-Friendly

Sustainable construction methods and energy-efficient systems minimize environmental impact.
